# Summary

# Strengths

- The problem is clearly motivated.
- The proposed method is easy to understand.

# Weaknesses

- The threat model could be explained more clearly.
- Some experimental settings need stronger justification.
- The paper could include more ablation studies.

# Questions for Authors

- Why was this parameter chosen?
- How does the method perform under stronger attacks?
- What are the main limitations in real-world deployment?
